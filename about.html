The hardest part of writing is getting started.

drafty makes it easier to produce your first draft by hiding your writing as you go. No self-editing, no hand-wringing. Save that for later and just get words on the page now.

Once you've written a first draft here, copy the text and take it to your word processor to turn it into something you love.
